About the Project

Identify and theme for your dataset

As a theme for my dataset I will look at collecting information about Metallica discography. To do this I will first collect information about each of the albums, this includes, but is not limited to: album artwork (and alternative album artwork), band members on the album, total playtime, number of songs, etc.
I will also provide information about each of the songs individually. I will provide more metadata about the songs individual song play time and song name. After this, I will include a link to an audio track of the song, I will not host the audio file itself (due to copyright) but will link to another website hosting the track legally, this website will likely be using a popular and widely available file format such as mp3 or wav. I will also include the musical tablature for the songs whether this be a hyperlink to an external site, an image, or a PDF.

Current manifestations of data about this theme

Some of this information has been curated in online websites. For example, the website http://www.metstuff.com/ has an excellent collection of vinyl records and CD’s from Metallica. This website has curated much information about each of their objects such as the artwork, track listings, and playtime. However, something they have not done is include any audio of the tracks, neither have they included any information about the members who were currently in the band at the time of release.
Another website at http://www.metpage.org/ has a lot of the information under their ‘discography’ section. When viewing the albums in this website they have a lot more information available, such as the album sales, band members, and song lyrics. However, this website does not seem to be complete as it is missing their most recent album. In addition to this, the formatting could be improved, for example the lyrics are all listed below the album information, this makes for a very long webpage and finding a certain song difficult. This could be improved by having the lyrics in separate tabs or with a hyperlinks.
